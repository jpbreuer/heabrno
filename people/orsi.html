POSITIONS:
 
 
2020-  Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Theoretical Physics and Astrophysics at Masaryk University, Brno, Czech Republic
 
2017-2019 SAO Predoctoral Fellow at Harvard-Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics, Cambridge, MA, USA

Orsi received her PhD for addressing the missing baryon problem, and uncovering the nature of ultra-diffuse galaxies using Chandra and XMM-Newton observations. Currently, she is a postdoctoral fellow in the High-Energy Astrophysics research group. Her research interests focus on galaxies, galaxy clusters, and the large-scale structure of the Universe through X-rays.
