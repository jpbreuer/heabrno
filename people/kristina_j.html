Kristýna is 1st year Master student of Astrophysics at Masaryk university in Brno. In her Master thesis she studies the perturbed motions of stars in the nuclear star cluster around the supermassive black hole in the Galactic center under the supervision of Michal Zajaček.

Kristýna is also interested in infrared and radio universe. For her Bachelor thesis she studied one of the radio galaxies (NGC 2329) with the Very Large Array and CASA software.
